BMI Calculator

About Us

We provide a simple tool to help you calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI), an indicator of body fat based on your height and weight. BMI is commonly used to categorize individuals into various weight categories that can help assess potential health risks.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
